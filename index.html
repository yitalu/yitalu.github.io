Hi, I am a PhD candidate in Political Science at Stony Brook University. My research interests include behavioral political economy, computational modeling, and cooperation in social dilemmas. I use game theory, agent-based modeling, and economic experiments to study topics like collective action problems, electoral competition, and effects of media bias. In addition, I study the relationship between political polarization and inequality in the American context, using time series analysis as my tool.
